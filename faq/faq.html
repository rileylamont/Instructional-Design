Frequently Asked Questions

Who should take this course?

This course is for anyone who struggles to stay motivated, focused, or consistent—especially when things get difficult. You will benefit if you’ve ever said:

“I want to work, but I just don’t feel like it.”
“I know what to do, I just can’t get myself to start.”
“I keep getting distracted and giving up.”
“I get overwhelmed, anxious, or discouraged when I try to focus.”

Most advice focuses on external tools, this course focuses on internal strategies that actually last.

What should I expect?

The course consists of four modules: Motivation, Emotion, Focus, and Habit. Each module consists of readings, videos, interactive elements, quizzes, templates, and opportunities for practice and self-reflection.

What will I Learn in this course?

This course will teach you how to remove limiting thoughts and beliefs, regulate negative emotions, mitigate internal distractions, and remove ego-driven pride.

How long does this course take?

This course takes about 4 hours to complete, but the longer you spend reflecting and applying what you learned, the more beneficial it will be.

Does online learning really work?

Yes! But you get out of it what you put into it. Fully engage with the course content to maximize outcomes.

How much does the course cost?

For you, it is free! You will pay this small amount of time invested, and it will pay you back for the rest of your life!!

What does “A Scientific Approach” mean?

It means that everything in the course is based on peer-reviewed psychological and educational research. In other words, I may share my experience, but my opinions are not present anywhere in the course.
