Mindset

Research shows that reflecting on and writing down your thoughts can significantly improve your ability to remember and apply what you have learned. Take a few minutes to thoughtfully respond to the prompts below to get the most out of this module.

1. Why is it important to be aware of your thoughts?

2. When you observe a negative thought, how should you respond?

3. What are the potential consequences of maintaining a negative mindset?
