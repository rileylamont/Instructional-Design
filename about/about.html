About this coures

The Cultivating Work Ethic course will help you remove barriers to success, starting with removing any limiting mindsets, regulating emotions, mitigating internal distractions, and uprooting ego-driven pride; it will help you develop the knowledge and skills needed to achieve great things regardless of their difficulty.

Instructor Biography

My name is Riley Peterson, I have been a science teacher for four years and am passionate about fostering student growth. I am currently pursuing a Doctorate of Education in Instructional Design and Technology and am hoping to help you remove unnecessary influences that hurt your ability to accomplish great things.
